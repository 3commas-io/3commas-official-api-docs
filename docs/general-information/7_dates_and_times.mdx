| Time and date | ISO 8601 |
| ----------|---------|
| 2024-08-23 | 2024-08-23T00:00:00.000Z |
| 01.10.2024 00:00:00 | 2024-10-01T04:00:00.000Z |
| 12 Sen 2024, 15:53:22 | 2024-09-12T15:53:22.940Z |

  Sells all assets on the exchange account in USD, creating orders on the exchange

:::info[ Additional Information]
  This endpoint works for the following exchanges:
    *Binance Spot*, *Binance Margin*, *Binance US*, *Coinbase Advanced Spot*, *Gemini Spot*, *Kraken Spot*, *Bybit Spot*, *GateIo*, *OKX Spot*, *Huobi Spot*, *Bitfinex Spot*, *Kucoin Spot*, *Bitstamp Spot*
:::

:::danger
When executing this request, all trading entities (bots, SmartTrade, trades, deals, and ordersâ€”except for Signal Bot) will be stopped and closed both in 3Commas and on the exchange.
:::
